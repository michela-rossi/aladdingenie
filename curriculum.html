Appearences and filmography

I’m a powerful, kind-hearted, cheerful, adventurous, and funny Genie. I’m loyal, wise, I love freedom and exploiting my powers for good purposes. I often tell jokes, I’m really good at singing, dancing and at doing impressions. Besides my bold personality I was gifted with nearly unlimited cosmic power.

Powers and abilities

I can easily break the laws of nature, shape-shift into anything, break the fourth wall, warp reality at a whim, lift the palace of Agrabah with no problems, give other people my power, undo the works of any magic (though mixing my magic with someone else's had consequences), and escape a magically sealed cave. I have a nearly unlimited amount of magical knowledge and I can also flawlessly imitate the voices and appearances of others.
